Outfitter for All Seasons!

This is a web page that will demonstrate how to use Javascript's Date Object and jQuery to change the page according to season.

Feel free to toggle between each hyperlink at the top of the page, or enter a date to reveal a different season.

Seasonal Months are as follows:

  • Spring: March - May
  • Summer: June - August
  • Fall: September - November
  • Winter: December - February
